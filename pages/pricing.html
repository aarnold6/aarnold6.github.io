Straightforward pricing for all ages. Reach out directly to check availability and schedule your first session.

Session Rates

Pricing

First sessions include an intake consultation. All sessions are provided in-person in the Clarkston, MI area. In-home appointments are available — fees apply and on a limited basis.

🌳
Adults
approx 60 minutes

Follow-Up Sessions $100
🌱 🌿
Kids & Infants
30 or 60 Minutes
First Session $100
60-Minute Session $100
30-Minute Session $50
Siblings: Each additional sibling seen in the same visit is billed at the standard session rate — no additional first-session fee per child.
Pricing is based on cash payment. Unfortunately, credit cards and insurance are NOT accepted. Receipts available upon request.

Booking

How to Schedule

Scheduling is simple — reach out directly by email and Jacquelynn will get back to confirm availability and session details.

01

Reach Out

Send an email to J.ArnoldCFT@gmail.com with your name, who the session is for, and a brief description of what brings you in.

02

Confirm Details

Jacquelynn will respond to confirm availability, session location, and answer any questions you have before your visit.

03

Complete Forms

First-time clients will be emailed consent and intake forms in advance — this helps make your first session as efficient as possible.

Policy

Cancellation Policy

Please review the cancellation policy before booking your first session.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

Life happens — and flexibility is understood, especially for families with young children. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please reach out as early as possible so the time can be offered to another client.

A specific cancellation window and any associated fees will be communicated when your appointment is confirmed. Questions about the policy can be directed to J.ArnoldCFT@gmail.com.
